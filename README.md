# 📊 End-to-End E-Commerce Business Analysis Dashboard in Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce business performance using KPIs, charts, and filters. This project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights to support data-driven decision making.

##  Project Objective

The objective of this project is to monitor and evaluate business performance across revenue, orders, product categories, cities, customer contribution, and payment methods through a clean and interactive dashboard.

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Pivot Charts  
- Slicers (Interactive Filters)  
- KPI Cards  
- Data Cleaning  
- Dashboard Design  

##Dashboard Features

✔ Revenue KPI  
✔ Orders KPI  
✔ Quantity KPI  
✔ Average Order Value (AOV)  
✔ Revenue by Category  
✔ Orders by City  
✔ Monthly Trend Analysis  
✔ Top 10 Customers  
✔ Revenue by Payment Method  
✔ Interactive Filters  

# Key Insights

### 1. Revenue is concentrated in a few product categories
A limited number of categories contribute significantly to overall business revenue.

### 2. Order distribution is balanced across cities
Customer demand appears relatively distributed across multiple locations.

### 3. Quantity sold remains strong
A total of **293,159 units** indicates healthy transaction activity.

### 4. Revenue trends fluctuate monthly
Sales performance shows variation throughout the year, indicating seasonal buying patterns.

### 5. Customer contribution is uneven
Top customers contribute a larger share of revenue compared to the remaining customer base.

### 6. Payment preferences impact revenue
Different payment methods show different revenue contributions, highlighting customer purchasing behavior.


#  Recommendations

### 1. Focus marketing on top-performing categories
Increase promotions and inventory allocation for categories generating the highest revenue.

### 2. Improve low-performing months
Launch seasonal campaigns and discounts during periods with lower sales.

### 3. Build customer retention strategies
Introduce loyalty programs and personalized offers for repeat customers.

### 4. Expand in strong-performing cities
Increase targeting and availability in cities with consistent order volume.

### 5. Optimize payment experience
Promote preferred payment methods to improve checkout efficiency.

### 6. Continue dashboard automation
Enhance reporting using Power Query and dynamic refresh mechanisms.
